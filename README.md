**Overview:**
A Web/Mobile Application designed to support environmental conservation programs, reforestation initiatives, and community-driven sustainability projects. The system enables organizations, barangays, volunteers, and environmental agencies to monitor tree planting activities, track environmental progress, and manage eco-projects efficiently in one centralized platform.

### Tech Stack:

* **Type:** Web / Mobile Application
* **Maps Integration:** Google Maps / Mapbox
* **Focus:** Environmental Monitoring & Sustainability
* **Goal:** Community Engagement & Ecological Restoration

### Key Features:

* Tree planting event scheduling and volunteer registration
* Interactive maps showing planting locations and green zones
* Tree growth monitoring with image uploads and status tracking
* Environmental reports and analytics dashboard
* QR code-based tree identification and monitoring
* Announcement and awareness campaign management
* Admin panel for managing volunteers, reports, and activities

## 2. Barangay Complaint System — Community Concern & Incident Reporting Platform

**Overview:**
A Web-Based Barangay Complaint Management System developed to improve communication between residents and barangay officials. The platform allows citizens to digitally submit complaints, incidents, and community concerns while enabling barangay administrators to efficiently monitor, resolve, and archive reports.

### Tech Stack:

* **Type:** Web Application
* **Focus:** Complaint Tracking & Community Service
* **Goal:** Faster Response & Organized Records Management

### Key Features:

* Online complaint submission with category selection
* Resident account registration and authentication
* Complaint status tracking (Pending, Ongoing, Resolved)
* File/image attachment for evidence submission
* SMS/Email notification updates for residents
* Admin dashboard for complaint management and reporting
* Incident history and archived records system
* Analytics and statistics for barangay reports and cases
